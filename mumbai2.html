chhatrapati shivaji maharaj terminus

Formerly called Victoria Terminus in British times, this marvellous railway station is among the historical monuments of Maharashtra for its architectural beauty, designed in stone with Victorian and Gothic influences. Being the headquarters of the Central Railways, it is from here that most local and outstation trains take off. Once here, you will notice that Mumbai truly never sleeps, with the constant hustle of travellers and the consistent calls of hawkers lining the platforms. Quite a few scenes from popular Bollywood movies have also been shot here. On different occasions, the facade of the station is beautifully lit to commemorate the occasion, which is quite a sight!
